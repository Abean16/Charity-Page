About The Cause:

Our mission is to make a splash for cleaner waters and healthier wildlife. Half of all proceeds from the Duck Race support the Clearwater Streams & Shores Initiative, a community-driven effort to remove trash, restore habitats, and protect our local waterways. The other half goes to Quack Haven Duck Rescue & Rehabilitation, where injured and orphaned ducks receive expert care before returning to the wild. Together, we're racing toward a future where our lakes, rivers, and feathered friends can thrive.
